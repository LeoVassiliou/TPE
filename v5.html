📈 Bayesian Reasoning

This calculator uses Bayes' theorem to properly update probabilities:

P(H|E) = P(E|H) × P(H) / P(E)
Posterior = Likelihood × Prior / Evidence

Every argument you rate becomes evidence E that updates the probability of theism H. Strong theistic evidence increases P(God|Evidence), while strong atheistic evidence decreases it.

How it works:

  1. Rate your confidence in each premise (0-100%)
  2. Arguments become Bayes factors for/against theism
  3. All evidence combines multiplicatively
  4. Final probability emerges from the mathematics
